Rare Rabbit (Founder : Manish Poddar)

Image Source: Your Image Source

"Fashion is the armor to survive the reality of everyday life." – Bill Cunningham

Rare Rabbit, under the umbrella of the House of Rare, has emerged as a disruptor in India's men’s fashion space. With a unique blend of European aesthetics and Indian sensibilities, it offers much more than clothes — it sells identity, aspiration, and confidence.

What Makes Rare Rabbit Stand Out?

  • Premium Positioning – Unlike fast fashion giants, Rare Rabbit targets the aspirational, style-conscious male audience with a boutique brand feel.
  • In-House Design and Storytelling – Their campaigns feel like editorials, crafted to evoke emotions and lifestyle aspirations.
  • Direct-to-Consumer Power – With a strong online presence and retail expansion in key Indian cities, they maintain brand control and customer connection.
  • Experience-First Retail – Their store interiors and product displays enhance the shopping experience, turning customers into loyalists.

What You Can Learn

Rare Rabbit’s journey teaches us the power of niche branding, how to use storytelling in product marketing, and the importance of blending global taste with local roots. It’s a case of smart design meeting savvy business.

Takeaway

In a crowded market, Rare Rabbit chose to play the premium game — and it's winning. If you're building a consumer brand, especially in fashion or lifestyle, there's a lot to steal (like an artist) from Rare Rabbit’s playbook.

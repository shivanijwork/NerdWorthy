Shivani Jain

Hi, I'm Shivani — the curious mind behind NerdWorthy. A techie by degree, a storyteller by heart, and a lifelong collector of strange facts, unfinished thoughts, vintage feels, and Google tabs. I write about what fascinates me — from forgotten rivers in deserts to the psychology of startups, and everything in between. When I’m not solving data puzzles or learning something new, you’ll find me wandering through old books, staring at skies, or building my next "just because" project. This blog is my digital attic of curiosity — filled with things I’d love to share with another nerd like you. Let's be nerds together.
Because curiosity doesn’t ask for permission — it just wanders.
